Twisted Wonderland

Twisted Wonderland is a game inspired by classic Disney villains. You're playing as character who was living in the "normal" world who is then chosen by the carriage to be a student at Night Raven College.

There are seven dorms that are inspired by the villains, such as, Jafar, Hades, The Evil Queen, The Queen of Hearts, Maleficent, Scar, and Ursula. They are knowns as "The Great Seven" and aren't seen as villains in there world but are viewed as great beings instead. However, all is not what it seems.
